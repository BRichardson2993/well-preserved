Pop Quiz

Which of the following may help you pay for an assisted living facility?

  • Disability insurance
  • Medicare
  • Private health insurance
  • All of the above
  • None of the above

Unfortunately, costs for services at an assisted living facility or adult day service program are not covered by private health insurance, disability insurance, Medicare, or private Medigap insurance. The correct answer is None of the above. A long-term plan may help you cover these costs.

If you have not prepared yourself for the life events like illness, retirement living or assisted living costs, we can help!
